  • What are the Golden Records?

    The Voyager Golden Records are two phonograph records that were included aboard both Voyager spacecraft launched in 1977. The records contain sounds and images selected to portray the diversity of life and culture on Earth, and are intended for any intelligent extraterrestrial life form who may find them.

    Inspired by the song Whitey on the Moon, by Gil-Scott Heron, this website aims to use the Golden Records as a means of information, but to critique the United States Government's decision to prioritise funding the Space Race above their own people. Each section of information assumes the role of a 'track' on the record.
